What ceramic coating actually does

Makes your paint dramatically easier to clean

Dirt, bird droppings, tree sap, and bug guts sit on the surface instead of bonding into your clear coat. Washing takes half the effort, and contaminants that would normally stain bare paint in hours take much longer to cause damage.

Slows UV damage and oxidation

Coatings contain UV-absorbing compounds that measurably reduce fading and oxidation — especially on red, black, and single-stage paints that are most vulnerable. It's not a force field. It's meaningful, proven protection.

Keeps that just-detailed look for years, not weeks

A properly applied coating maintains gloss and depth far longer than wax or sealant. You'll notice it every time it rains and water sheets off instead of clinging. Most vehicles look freshly detailed between washes for 3 to 5 years.
