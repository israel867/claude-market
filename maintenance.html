What Every Maintenance Visit Includes

Exterior

  • Full hand wash & foam bath
  • Wheels & tires cleaned
  • Tire dressing
  • Spray sealant applied (protects paint between visits)
  • Door & trunk jambs wiped

Interior

  • Full interior wipe down (dash, console, doors)
  • Light vacuum
  • Floor mats cleaned
  • Windows inside & out
  • Air freshener

Why Maintenance Detailing?

Without maintenance:
  • Dirt and contaminants eat into your clear coat
  • Sealant and wax wear off in weeks
  • You need a full detail every time — more expensive
  • Your car spends most of the year looking dirty
With maintenance:
  • Paint stays protected with fresh sealant every visit
  • Contaminants never get a chance to bond
  • Full details needed less often — saves money long term
  • Your car looks clean 365 days a year
