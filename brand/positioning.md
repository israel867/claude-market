## Positioning Statement

We are the detailing shop that tells you exactly what ceramic coating does, what it doesn't do, what it costs, and what your warranty actually covers — before you spend a dollar.

---

## Core Message & Angle

**Angle:** Radical Transparency

The ceramic coating industry is drowning in hype — scratch-proof claims, meaningless hardness ratings, fine-print warranties, and upsell traps. Every other detailer is racing to out-promise each other. We do the opposite.

We lead with honesty. We tell customers what coatings actually do, set expectations they can trust, price clearly with no hidden fees, and never push maintenance products to protect a product that should already be protecting their car. In a market where trust has been destroyed by overpromising, we win by under-promising and over-delivering.

**We don't sell coatings. We sell confidence.**

## Target Audience Profile

**Primary: The Skeptical Second-Timer**
- 30-55 years old, higher income ($80K+)
- Has either gotten a coating before and felt misled, or researched coatings and got overwhelmed by conflicting claims
- Reads forums, watches YouTube comparisons, checks Reddit threads
- Distrusts anything that sounds too good to be true
- Values straight talk over sales polish
- Willing to pay premium for a provider they actually believe

**Secondary: The Research-Heavy First-Timer**
- Considering their first ceramic coating
- Has seen the wild claims and doesn't know who to trust
- Their biggest objection isn't price — it's fear of being ripped off
- Wants someone to explain it plainly without a sales pitch

**Tertiary: The Pragmatic Car Owner**
- Doesn't care about detailing culture or car enthusiasm
- Just wants their vehicle protected without being sold a lifestyle
- Values clarity, convenience, and no ongoing obligations

## Top 3 Pain Points We Address

### 1. "I don't know what's real anymore"
The market is flooded with 9H/10H hardness claims, "scratch-proof" promises, and "never wash your car again" marketing. Customers feel gaslit. We cut through the noise with plain-language explanations of what ceramic coatings actually do and — critically — what they don't.

### 2. "I'm afraid the warranty is designed to be voided"
Most coating warranties are loaded with loopholes: required maintenance products, mandatory annual inspections, proprietary wash soaps. Customers feel the warranty exists to protect the business, not them. We explain warranty terms upfront in plain English and never condition coverage on buying more from us.

### 3. "I don't want to be upsold after I already paid"
Customers pay $1,000-$3,000 and then get told they need quarterly SiO2 top-offs, specific maintenance kits, and annual check-ins — or their protection degrades. We set honest maintenance expectations from day one and never manufacture dependency on additional purchases.

## What We Are NOT

- **We are NOT a hype shop.** We don't use "revolutionary," "ultimate," or "game-changer" to describe a coating application.
- **We are NOT brand-loyal to one coating line.** We recommend the right product for your vehicle, driving conditions, and budget — not whatever gives us the best dealer margin.
- **We are NOT a maintenance-trap business.** We don't sell you a coating and then sell you products to protect the coating.
- **We are NOT a jargon shop.** We don't throw around 9H hardness ratings, nano-ceramic buzzwords, or SiO2 percentages to sound impressive. We explain what matters in terms you actually understand.
- **We are NOT trying to be everything.** We don't promise scratch-proof, self-healing, never-wash-again magic. We tell you exactly what you're getting.

## Tone of Voice Direction

### We Sound Like:
- A knowledgeable friend who happens to be a detailer — direct, confident, zero fluff
- Someone who respects your intelligence and your wallet
- Calm authority, not loud salesman

### Voice Attributes:
| Attribute | What It Means | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Direct** | Say it plainly. No filler, no hedging. | "Ceramic coatings don't prevent scratches. They make your paint easier to clean and harder to stain." |
| **Honest** | Admit limitations before the customer discovers them. | "This coating lasts 3-5 years with proper washing. Anyone telling you 10 years isn't being straight." |
| **Confident** | We don't need hype because we know our work. | "We'll show you the prep, the application, and the result. The work speaks." |
| **Respectful** | Never talk down. Never pressure. | "Here's what I'd recommend and why. Take your time." |
| **Anti-corporate** | Human, not polished. Real, not produced. | "We're a detail shop, not a marketing department. Ask us anything." |

### We Never:
- Use superlatives without proof ("best," "ultimate," "#1")
- Pressure with urgency or scarcity tactics ("limited slots!", "book now before prices go up!")
- Hide behind jargon to sound technical
- Trash competitors by name — we differentiate by what we *do*, not what others don't
- Overpromise and then rely on fine print to cover ourselves

### Writing Rules:
1. If a sentence sounds like it could be on any detailer's website, rewrite it
2. If we can't prove a claim, we don't make it
3. Lead with what the customer needs to know, not what we want to say
4. Short sentences. Plain words. No fluff.
5. When in doubt, say less
